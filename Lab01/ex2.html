Identifiers

Identifiers are unique codes or numbers that are used to identify a person, object, or entity. Here are some kinds of identifiers:

Citizen Identification Number

Citizen Identification Number is a twelve-digit identifier assigned by the The Police Department For Administrative Management Of Social Order to Vietnamese citizens. It is used to track general, identification and authentication information of the citizen. The format of a Citizen Identification Number is XXX-XXX-XXX-XXX, where X is a digit.

Example: 038245000689

Student ID Number

A Student ID Number is a unique identifier assigned to students by their educational institution. It is used to track enrollment, grades, and academic records. The format of a Student ID Number varies by institution.

Example: 20192020

Email Address

An email address is a unique identifier assigned to each user of an email service. It is used to send and receive emails. The format of an email address is username@domain.com, where username is the name of the user and domain.com is the name of the email service.

Example: example@gmail.com

Vehicle Identification Number

A Vehicle Identification Number (VIN) is a unique identifier assigned to every vehicle. It is used to track vehicle ownership, registration, and history. The format of a VIN is 17 characters long and includes both letters and numbers.

Example: 1G1ND52F15M000000

Passport Number

A Passport Number is a unique identifier assigned to each passport holder. It is used to verify the identity and nationality of the holder. The format of a Passport Number varies by country.

Example: A1234567
